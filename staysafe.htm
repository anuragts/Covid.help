4)Bursting of cells makes pus which cover the site for exchange of gases.

5)Advancded immune system already starts acting to produce antibodies.

7)You can now understand the symptoms-Cough to take the pus out of alveoli,Breathing problems due to lack of oxygen,fever because your body is fighting.
