How to stay safe during these times

It's now 1 year since this pandemic started,so many of you have already watched/read about how to stay safe.
Still here is ways to stay safe.
We will also cover the topic what to do after infection.


1)Best solution to stay safe is not leaving your house at all.But for some things you need to go out,like for food.Only go out when its very important.

2)If you are going out make sure you wear you mask properly and must carry a sanitizer. 3)Avoid touching any surfaces,avoid crowds.Make sure to sanitize your hands after touching any thing.

4)After returning home,immediately put your clothes in washing machine and wash your mouth,hand and legs with soap.Throw or wash your mask properly.

5)Get vaccinated as soon as possible.There isn't a side effect of the vaccine it's just a rumour.

Now you have ample knowledge of how to stay safe.But you may get infection if you dont follow these rules nicely.So without further ado let me tell you what to do after you get symptoms or you get infected.



What if you have symptoms or get infected-:

1)Dont panic it may or may not be a seasonal fever.Take basic meds like Paracetamol or refer doctor for basic meds.

2)Do breathing exercises and normal body exercises to keep yourself fit.

3)Go to hospital if symptoms stays or you feel like you are infected.

4)Still maintain proper distance with other people and wear a mask to make sure you dont spread the infection to others.

5)Do what your doctor recommends you to do.



Many patients in India are not able to find bed in hospitals for treatment or they cant afford high prices treatment.For them they should follow these.

1)Treat yourself with Ayurvedic medicines or herbs.

2)Eat food timely dont make yourself weak.

3)Have a good mentality for fighting this virus.

4)Do breathing exercise constantly like Anulom Vilom.

5)Stay away from other people.
